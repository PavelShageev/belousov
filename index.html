Who we are

A11 Refrigeration is the commercial refrigeration specialists.

We can install, maintain and repair your commercial refrigeration equipment. A11 Refrigeration is certified with the Australian Refrigeration Council Ltd (ARC tick) and compliant with all Government Regulations and relevant Australian Standards to include the Ozone Protection and Synthetic Greenhouse Gas Management Act.

What we do

Commercial Refrigeration Service & Repair

Our service vehicles are always stocked with most commonly used spare parts to avoid extra costs and delays in repairs. All commercial freezer and fridge repairs are completed to a high standard of quality and conform to manufacturers specifications.

A11 Maintenance Program

All refrigeration systems depreciate with time. If not maintained properly, it is progressively losing efficiency and consuming more power. Far to often the main reason for failure of refrigeration units are due to poor maintenance.

A11 recommends all commercial refrigeration be serviced by a professional technician at least once per year. For a set fee per year we offer you regular servicing which keep your refrigeration equipment running smoothly and advanced warnings when refrigeration unit needs further attention, before it breakdowns.
